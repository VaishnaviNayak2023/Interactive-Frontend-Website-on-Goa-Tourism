featured places

Tambdi Surla, Goa

Nestled in the Western Ghats, this 12th-century Kadamba-style temple is made of basalt and surrounded by dense forests.

Vagator Beach, Goa

Known for its dramatic red cliffs, trance culture, and sunset views, Vagator is perfect for both solitude seekers and party lovers.

Mangeshi Temple, Goa

Dedicated to Lord Shiva, this 400-year-old temple is a serene blend of Goan architecture.

Basilica of Bom Jesus, Goa

This UNESCO World Heritage Site is revered for its baroque architecture and spiritual significance.

Dudhsagar Waterfall, Goa

Falling from a height of over 300 meters, these ‘sea of milk’ waterfalls are a breathtaking sight during the monsoons.

Fort Aguada, Goa

This 17th-century Portuguese fort offers panoramic views of the sea and a historic lighthouse.
